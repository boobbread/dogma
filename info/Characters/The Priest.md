## Core Drive
- **Core Want:**  
  To help the people of the village by whatever means necessary, even if they stray from his beliefs.

- **Core Fear:**  
  That he gets tried for heresy, but not because he would die but because he would have to leave the village and let the people die.

- **Internal Conflict:**  
  He wants to help, but he knows that it will drawn him further from god, risking his position in the church along with his newfound friendship with [[The Vicar]].

> One-sentence summary:  
> **He wants to be a symbol of hope and vitality for the people and ensure they all see the next day, but achieving it risks everything he knows - his livelihood, his faith, his upbringing.**
